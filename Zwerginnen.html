Zwerginnen

Zwerginnen is a single player first person shooter where the player's objective is to kill all of the rouge robots within an abandoned factory. To accomplish their task the player must run and gun as they kill a variety of enemies to earn coins that can be used to obtain better weapons from vending machines.

Using C++ and blueprint in the Unreal 5 engine, our team tasked ourselves with designing, developing, and building a game prototype in one school semester.

The Player

When making the player character for the game I decided to give the player an advantage over the enemies, in an effort to make the gmae more fun. To achieve this the player's weapons would use a raycast, or line trace in Unreal, when fighting the enemies so that the player could more easily shot and hit enemies while on the move, compared to aiming and shooting bullet with simulated physics, velocity and travel time.
