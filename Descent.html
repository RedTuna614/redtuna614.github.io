Descent

Project Descent is a 3D sci-fi, fps dungeon crawler with rogue like elements, where the player must complete a variety of missions in an unexplored alien vault to earn as many credits as possible before meeting their untimely demise in the vault.

Currently the game is very early in development and is being developed by myself in the Unreal 5.4 engine using a combination of C++ and blueprint.

Dungeon Generation

When making the the generation system, I took inspiration from how levels are generated in, Warframe where it seems that the level is generated from a selection of prebuilt rooms that are placed and connected at random. To make the system I have each room store the transforms of where it's neighbors will connect to the room, and either a door or dead end will spawn, and separated the rooms in different types, halls, chambers, stairs, goal, and start. Since each level needs a starting room, I made the generator create the start room first, and then generate the rest of the level up the given size. The size of level being the number of rooms generated.
